About Fiona's Breed

Fiona is a Miniature Schnauzer mixed with Shih Tzu. She's pretty hypoallergenic, doesn't really shed, and weighs about 20 lbs. Her fur grows like hair and needs trimming every one to two months — in winter she keeps it long to stay warm.

She gets most of her looks from her Schnauzer side and her fur texture from her Shih Tzu side. She also has that classic Schnauzer instinct — she's been known to herd rodents into a corner, though she's never quite figured out what to do next.
